# Digital Nutrition Label

**What if your screen time had a nutrition label?**

A proposal for mandatory, standardized, user-facing behavioral transparency APIs for every algorithmic service operating in the EU.

🌍 **Website**: [digitalnutritionlabel.eu](https://digitalnutritionlabel.eu)

## The idea

We regulate every product that enters your body — food, medicine, cosmetics, tobacco. But algorithmic services that shape the information diet of 450 million Europeans have no comparable user-facing transparency.

The Digital Nutrition Label proposes a simple principle: any algorithmic service operating in the EU must expose, per user, via a standardized REST API, what content was served, what was sponsored, what behavioral signals were used, and what revenue was generated.

Don't ban anything. Just turn the lights on.
